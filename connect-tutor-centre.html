Tutor Centre Connection

Connect your learner to a Tutor Centre

Enter the short code your tutor centre shared with you. Once connected, your tutor centre can support the selected learner and view their relevant LearnerGenie activity.

How it works

This connects one learner profile, not the whole parent account. If you have more than one learner, choose the correct learner before connecting.

  • ✓Only the selected learner profile is linked.
  • ✓The tutor centre can remove learners who are not part of their centre.
  • ✓Your learner profile and saved work are not deleted if the connection is removed.

Choose the learner who attends this tutor centre.

You can type the code or open this page from a tutor centre QR link.
